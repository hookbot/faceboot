Rules:

This is a short list of the CPPS.me rules - there aren't many but the ones we do have are intended to keep our users safe.

  • No abusing bugs We do not allow the abuse and exploitation of bugs or any means of reverse engineering CPPS.me for malicious purposes.
  • No sharing personal information We do not allow sharing of personal information, of yourself or others. This is intended to keep you safe!
  • Absolutely no advertising Advertising any other CPPS or malicious/adult related websites is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.
  • Harassment, racism, bullying We do not allow the harassment or bullying of other players. Please refrain from taking part of this kind of activity or your account could be banned.
  • Sexual activity Sexual content and "cybering" is not allowed in public rooms. If you must please take it to an igloo.

Breaking any of these rules could result in a mute, kick or ban at the moderator's discretion. Please do your best to obey them.
